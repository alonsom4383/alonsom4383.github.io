Soft white sand, warm weather, and vibrant shades of bright blue and teal—the magnificent coast of Cancun has all the elements that define the perfect beach. All along Cancun’s 13 miles of coast, I have had the chance to visit various beaches that look like they were taken straight out of a commercial. In fact, the entire Hotel Zone is filled with stretches of sand that are ideal for relaxing on a beach towel, sipping on a margarita, splashing around in the Caribbean Sea, and working on an enviable tan.
